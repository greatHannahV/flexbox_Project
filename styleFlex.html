Mooncalf
The Mooncalf was a shy magical beast that only came out of its burrow during a full moon.
The Mooncalf had smooth, pale grey skin, and four spindly legs that ended in large flat webbed feet. The Mooncalf also had a very long neck and bulging blue eyes that sat on the top of its head.
History

1920s

Jacob Kowalski feeding Newt Scamander's Mooncalves Newt Scamander had a herd of Mooncalves in his suitcase during his visit to New York in 1926.He also kept another small group in the basement of his apartment. However, it is possible that these were part of the same herd.
